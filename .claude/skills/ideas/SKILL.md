# Ideas Backlog

## Capturing Ideas

When an idea comes up during work:
1. Write it to `.planning/ideas.md` with date, description, and priority
2. Tag it: `enhancement`, `optimization`, `feature`, `debt`, `research`
3. Don't act on it immediately — capture and continue current work

## Reviewing Ideas

Periodically review the ideas backlog:
1. Remove duplicates
2. Promote urgent items to GSD phases
3. Archive completed or irrelevant ideas

## Priority Levels

- **P0**: Blocking current work — handle now
- **P1**: Needed for current milestone — plan into a phase
- **P2**: Nice to have — backlog for next milestone
- **P3**: Someday/maybe — revisit quarterly
