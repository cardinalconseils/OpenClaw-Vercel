Stop asking. Diagnose the situation and make a decision.

Decision framework (in order of priority):
1. **Safety** — Will this break production or lose data? If yes, choose the safe option.
2. **Simplest** — What's the simplest solution that works? Choose that.
3. **Convention** — What do most projects do? Follow convention.
4. **Reversible** — If the decision is easily reversible, just pick one and move.

Apply this to: $ARGUMENTS

State your decision clearly, explain the reasoning in one sentence, and execute it.
